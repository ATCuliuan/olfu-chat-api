DOCUMENT TITLE: OLFU Quezon City (QC) Campus Commuter Guide
LOCATION: The OLFU QC Campus is located at #1 Esperanza St., Hilltop Mansion Heights, Lagro, Quezon City. It is very close to SM Fairview and Robinsons Novaliches.

ROUTE 1: Coming from Commonwealth Avenue / Philcoa / Quezon Memorial Circle
- Ride any Jeepney, UV Express, or Bus with the signboard "SM Fairview" or "Lagro" or "Palmer".
- Ask the driver to drop you off at the intersection near the Hilltop area / Regalado Highway before reaching SM Fairview.
- From the main road, you can walk to the campus gates or take a short tricycle ride.

ROUTE 2: Coming from Novaliches Bayan
- Ride a jeepney or UV Express bound for "SM Fairview".
- Alight at SM Fairview or Robinsons Novaliches.
- From there, ride a jeepney going to "Lagro" or take a tricycle directly to the OLFU Hilltop campus.

ROUTE 3: Coming from MRT-3 (Quezon Avenue Station / Centris)
- Go to the UV Express terminal or bus stop below the MRT station.
- Ride a UV Express or Bus heading to "SM Fairview".
- Drop off near the Lagro / Hilltop area before the malls, and walk or take a tricycle to the campus.

ROUTE 4: Coming from SJDM, Bulacan (Muzon / Tungko)
- Ride a bus or jeepney heading south to "SM Fairview".
- Alight at SM Fairview.
- Cross the highway or take a short jeepney ride towards Lagro to reach the campus.

TRICYCLE FARES: Regular tricycle fares apply from the main highway to the campus gates, usually ranging from 15 to 30 pesos depending on if you share the ride (special vs. regular).
